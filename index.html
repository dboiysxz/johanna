Johanna Ulfsak is an artist and textile designer based in Tallinn, Estonia.

contact

Her work is presented as artworks in gallery exhibitions and as non-seasonal collections of design and fashion objects. Ulfsak combines and mixes traditional weaving techniques with contemporary art concepts to create unique items and installations that challenge the established boundaries between disciplines. Since 2020 she is a guest lecturer at Estonian Academy of Arts.
